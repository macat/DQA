About Smartlinky

Our project

The idea is to connect on-line tech documentation sites with sites that may enrich the documentation. For example, for each section of one documentation we can attached links to blog posts, twitter feeds, stackoverflow answers etc, regarding this section. Users will be able to add links or influence the order of existing ones. Users will be able to jump to external resource directly from the section they are interested in.
