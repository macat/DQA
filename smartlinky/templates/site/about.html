About Smartlinky

Our project

We all love well written docs. But it's not an easy task to produce such. How about letting people collaborate on this?

It's easy to start. Get our Chrome browser plugin [here]. It's 100% free of costs and the code is released on BSD-3 license.

Now... Have You been working on something recently that has on-line docs? Navigate to that page now. If the docs have a good structure (sections) then You are lucky. If not the try the ones [here].

Enable the plugin. Click on Smartlinky icon to see additional links by each section. They are added by people or taken from some Q&As sites. You can participate as well by adding links to blog posts, twitter feeds, stackoverflow answers etc. Whatever You consider as valuable information regarding the section You are now reading...

It's simple! :)
